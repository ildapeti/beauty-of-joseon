SMALL HAPPINESS CALENDAR LETTER_SEP

September 01, 2022

Have you ever heard of Hangawi?

Hangawi is another name for Chuseok, Korean Thanksgiving.
"Han" means big and "gawi" means the middle of autumn. So it means a big day in the middle of August. On Hangawi, you can see the biggest and most beautiful full moon of autumn.

The colorful, half-moon-shaped rice cake in the illustration is a traditional Korean rice cake called songpyeon.
The whole family gathered together to make songpyeon and eat it, giving thanks for the year's harvest.
Also, it is a long-held belief that if you wish upon the full moon drawn in the illustration, your dream will come true.

How about making your wishes come true while looking at the beautiful full moon ?

SMALL HAPPINESS CALENDAR LETTER_AUG

August 01, 2022

What do you think a hot summer day in August was like in Joseon?

There is a Korean proverb that says, "The gift for Dano is a fan, and the gift for the winter solstice is a calendar".
It means that it will soon be summer as Dano is nearing. During this time, fans with handwriting or drawing on them were gifted as a wish for a cool summer.

The illustration on this fan depicts a scarlet campsis flower, which is commonly seen on Korean roads in summer.
The symbolic meanings of the campsis flower are "honor" and "longing".

How about sending this illustrated postcard to someone you miss so they can feel the meaning of the campsis flower? Do you have someone you miss, too?

SMALL HAPPINESS CALENDAR LETTER_JULY

July 01, 2022

Do you remember the holiday Dano that we introduced in our June feed?

In the past, women collected dew from irises to use as a toner and sometimes washed their hair in water in which irises had been boiled during Dano.

There is a famous painting called "Dano Pungjeong" by Shin Yun-bok that depicts women washing their hair using iris water on Dano.
We transformed this painting into a new illustration calendar with BOJ's own MOOD :)
If you look closely at the women in the illustration, you can see some tying up their hair while cleansing their faces.
Also, the pretty flower with a purple color is the iris flower. Back then, women prayed for only good things to happen while caring for their skin with iris.
